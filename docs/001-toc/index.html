    # Article What You'll Learn
    201 Twelve Ways to See Overview of all twelve fills, comparison table, decision flowchart, shared parameters.
    202 Linear: The Engraver's Line Parallel lines that evoke copperplate engraving, banknote illustration, and crosshatch.
    203 Wave: Rivers of Line Flowing sinusoidal curves for Art Nouveau, landscapes, fabric, and moiré effects.
    204 Circular: Concentric Rings Concentric rings radiating from a center point, mapping brightness to ring spacing.
    205 Spiral: The Continuous Wind A single continuous winding path from center outward, ideal for plotters and vinyl aesthetics.
    206 Radial: Exploding Rays Rays emanating from a center point like a sunburst, modulated by tone.
    207 Halftone: Dots and Shapes Dots, squares, stars, or custom SVG shapes arranged in a grid or scattered randomly.
    208 Trace: Finding Edges Edge-detection contour lines that follow the boundaries in your source image.
    209 Wireframe: Dimensional Lattice A 3D lattice mesh draped over your image, creating an elevation-map effect.
    210 Scribble: Controlled Chaos Chaotic, energetic strokes that cluster in dark regions and scatter in light ones.
    211 Fractal: Space-Filling Curves Peano curves that weave through your image with mathematical precision.
    212 Text: Painting with Letters Characters forming images, from typographic portraits to ASCII art in vector.
    213 Handmade: Your Strokes, Your Rules Draw or import your own strokes, then let Vexy Lines distribute them across the image.
    214 Combining Fills: The Art of the Stack Layering multiple fills with crosshatching, mixed media, tonal stacking, and overlap control.
